Protecting History

Marked on the map are all of the locations declared "Bien de Interes Cultural" in the city of Madrid. These locations are protected under the highest level from the government of the Communidad de Madrid. These are considered relevant elements in the history of art and architecture in Spain, or are they constitute a "milestone in the urban fabric" of the city. Official list here

Locations

There are over 200 locations in the city of Madrid that are listed as BIC. In addition to these locations, there are over 9,000 other properites around the city, with different levels of protection. BIC gaurentees the highest level, meaning that their architectural characteristics, construction, volumes, forms and decorative elements should be maintained.
