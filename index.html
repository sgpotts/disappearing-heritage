Behind the story

The city of Madrid has 226 monuments under the highest level of official protection for its historical and architectural value, according to a document by the Community of Madrid's Government. In addition to that number, there are over 9,000 protected buildings in the capital, according to the City Hall of Madrid. With so much historical and cultural heritage in the city, and the crisis causing spending cuts, the citizens of Madrid and its many tourists are losing valuable buildings, abandoned for years and on the verge of ruin. Can something be done?
